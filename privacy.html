Privacy Policy — Clinix360

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Clinix360 ("we", "us", "the app") is an internal business tool used by Clinix360 / DSkin dermatology clinics to manage daily cash reconciliation and to receive and respond to customer enquiries sent through Instagram Direct Messages. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

  • Instagram message data — when you send a Direct Message to our connected Instagram business account, we receive your Instagram-scoped user ID and the content of your message via the Meta (Instagram) Platform.
  • Lead records — we store these messages as enquiry ("lead") records so our clinic staff can read and reply to them.
  • Internal business data — daily cash totals and operational notes entered by clinic staff. This is not collected from members of the public.

2. How We Use Information

  • To read, organise, and reply to enquiries you send us on Instagram.
  • To provide customer service and follow up on appointment or treatment questions.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

3. Data Sharing

Your message data is processed through Meta Platforms (Instagram) and stored in our service providers used solely to operate this app: Supabase (database hosting) and Netlify (application hosting). These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain enquiry records for as long as needed to provide customer service and meet our business and legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time (see below).

5. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your message data, email dermadwarka@gmail.com with the Instagram handle you used to message us. We will delete your associated enquiry records within 30 days and confirm by email.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: dermadwarka@gmail.com
